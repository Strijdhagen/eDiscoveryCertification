Reveal-Brainspace Certification

Reveal and Brainspace merged in January of 2021 to become the “leading AI-Powered Platform”. Together with another acquisition NexLP, Reaveal-Brainspace offers a full range of eDiscovery services.

Brainspace is becoming an increasingly popular eDiscovery analytics platform, and offers a very thorough certification platform to go with it: Brainwaves. Several certificates are currently available for every level: Analyst, Specialist, Administrator, and Sales. Furthermore, Reveal introduced the Reveal Reviewer Certification in the beginning of 2021!

Brainspace Analyst Certification

The Brainspace Analyst certification teaches you how to use all of the major features of Brainspace as well as training you on the standard use cases and their associated workflows.

Before you can do the exam and get certified you need to complete the accompanying course. The curriculum contains subjects such as Brainspace basics, Visualisation Features, and Standard Use Cases & Workflow

Brainspace Specialist Certification

The Brainspace Specialist Certification is designed for individuals who are primarily responsible for the data ingestion process into Brainspace. This course will train users on how to manage Datasets, Users, Connectors, and perform basic troubleshooting of the Brainspace environment.

Before you can do the exam and get certified you need to complete the accompanying course. The curriculum contains subjects such as: Brainspace Architecture, Creating a Dataset, and Basic Troubleshooting

Reveal AI Certification

This online Reveal certification teaches you the fundamentals of Reveal's AI technology. All professionals are encouraged to take this course. The curriculum begins with a brief over of AI and the various types of artificial intelligence being used within the eDiscovery industry. You will then learn how to use Reveal's Search Intent AI and data visualizations. You will also learn how to build custom AI models. In addition, the course will teach you the basics of how to leverage pre-built models within the AI Model Library. The course concludes with an overview of Reveal's AI review enhancers including transcription, image labeling, and translation.
