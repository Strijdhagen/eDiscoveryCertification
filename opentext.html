OpenText Certification

OpenText is one of the biggest players in the world of eDiscovery since their strategical acquisitions the last couple of years, and now offers an end-to-end eDiscovery solution!
OpenText's eDiscovery products are Axcelrate, Insight, and Legal Hold, and there are currently 3 certificates available for you to acquire, one of which is the EDAX250 certificate, which is completely free!

OpenText EDAX150 - Axcelerate Data Processing Specialist Certification

This in-person certification course is intended for data processing and/or legal team members. These users will ingest data, conduct data processing, cull data and make it available for review in the Axcelerate Review and Analysis application.

More Information

Certificate Overview

  • Pricing

    $1500

  • Course Setup

    2 Day Course with a practical exam and 45 question quiz exam

  • Exam Duration

    1 Hours Online Exam and 3 Hour Practical Exam

  • Online or Testing Center?

    Both

  • Recommended Prerequisites

    Completion of the Axcelerate 5 Review and Analysis – Basic course is recommended.

OpenText EDAX200 - Axcelerate Review And Analysis - Case Manager

This in-person certification course is intended for Case Managers who will set up new Review and Analysis projects. This user might also manage the review team and review process once review batches have been created and will create productions.

OpenText EDAX250 - Axcelerate Review And Analysis – Investigator Certification

This guided lab session takes you through all the basic skills necessary to hit the ground running for your next investigation. You’ll learn about metadata filtering, phrase and keyword searching, visualizations, and structuring different analytics to rapidly find documents of interest.
