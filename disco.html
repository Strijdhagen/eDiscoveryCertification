DISCO Certification

DISCO is one of the fastest-growing legal tech companies in the industry, offering an end-to-end AI-powered eDiscovery solution for law firms, corporations, and government agencies.
With four new certifications, DISCO is taking their next step with the DISCO University program. Four different certificates are now available for you to add to your resume: Review Management, Search & Review, Data Management, and Project Management.

DISCO Certified Review Management

Are you the conductor when it comes to orchestrating all of the details to efficiently run a document review? This certification proves your mastery over:

  • Matter and review workflow management
  • DISCO AI and advanced optimization tactics
  • Search term reporting
  • Managing productions and exhibit sets
