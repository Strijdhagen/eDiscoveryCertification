EDT Certification

EDT is an E-Discovery provider that integrates processing, analytics, review, and production in a single platform, with a focus on cost-effectiveness and ease of use. The company offers several training and accreditation courses with the EDT Acadamy.

Certified EDT Reviewer (CER)

The EDT CER training and accreditation program is designed for those who will be using EDT to review documents.

Certified EDT Case Manager (CECM)

CECM builds upon the CER certification and designed for those who will be involved in case strategy and project management. This may include early case assessment, culling, analyzing, batching documents for review, reporting review progress and sampling. This role typically provides a liaison point between the legal team and the technical team.

Certified EDT Site Administrator (CESA)

CESA includes and builds upon all other certifications, this course is designed for those responsible for administration of the entire EDT environment. This includes installation, upgrades, configuring and maintaining the environment, deploying new processing servers, and monitoring usage logs and metrics.
