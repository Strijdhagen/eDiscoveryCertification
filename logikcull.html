Logikcull Certification

The San Francisco-based eDiscovery software company Logikcull focuses on hands-on DIY legal teams with their no-nonsense product: an instant eDiscovery solution that provides a powerfully simple legal software for processing, reviewing, and producing data.
Logikcull offers three levels of certification with the “Culler” certification program. The first level is 100% free!

Logikcull Certified - Level 1

The basic "Culler" level 1 certificate is free!

Cullers have mastered the basics of discovery. These legal professionals can filter documents by metadata, run simple and complex searches, create and apply tags, redact sensitive information, and even train others in the basics of discovery

Topics:

  • Simple and advanced search strategies
  • Grouping and filtering documents
  • Leveraging metadata
  • Search and review workflows
  • Quality control measures

Logikcull Certified Pro - Level 2

Pro Cullers can maximize Logikcull’s full capability. They can create file, cloud, and database uploads; customize reviews; filter documents through complex searches; and create custom production specifications and load files.

Topics:

  • Load files and database uploads
  • Cloud-to-cloud ingestion
  • Project set up and review management
  • Document filtering and batching
  • Producing documents

Logikcull Certified Master - Level 3

As the name implies, Master Cullers have mastered the discovery process. They not only meet Pro Culler competencies, but can implement quality controls, draft complex search strings, improve discovery processes through account level templates, and more.
