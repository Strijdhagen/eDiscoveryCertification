Relativity Certification

Relativity will almost need no introduction, one of the leaders in eDiscovery has an extensive certification and learning platform. Today Relativity offers 10 certificates, of which the “Relativity Certified Administrator” is one of the most popular (and difficult!) to obtain.

Relativity offers a certificate for almost every specialty imaginable such as processing, infrastructure, and analytics. The most recent certification is the RelativityOne Review Pro, specifically designed for review on Relativity’s cloud platform.

One of the more unique certificates is the Relativity Certified Training Pro course which is a very rigorous crash course to become a Relativity trainer!

Relativity Certified Administrator (RCA)

Relativity Certified Administrator (RCA) is Relativity's main certificate and one of the most popular certificates in the eDiscovery world. The certificate is an essential requirement for many eDiscovery and Litigation support job listings.

The exam requires you to understand most administrative aspects of Relativity which makes it not only hard to pass but also ensures that you will know the platform inside-and-out for the rest of your career

For this certificate, it's recommended to use to official study guide since there's a lot to cover! Relativity will also provide you with an "RCA Prep Environment" so you don't have to practice with your employer's instance.

The certificate exam has a notorious hands-on part on which many eDiscovery professionals have already stumbled, so make sure to prepare well!

Relativity specialist exams

The Relativity specialist exams are interesting for those that wish to specialize or are already specialists in certain aspects of the Relativity platform. You will also need to obtain 2 specialist exams + RCA to call yourself a “Relativity Expert“, and if you are really crazy about Relativity then you can attempt to become a “Relativity Master“. You will only need to complete 4 specialist exams in addition to your RCA to obtain master status!

Relativity Analytics Specialist

The Relativity Analytics Specialist exam covers everything analytics, from clustering to Relativity's famous active learning. Make sure to have a couple of projects with extensive analytics applied under your belt before you attempt this one!

Recommended Prerequisites

At least 6 Months of experience managing analytics projects. It's recommended to take 'Analytics and Active Learning' training

Relativity Project Management Specialis

The Relativity Project Management Specialist certificate is specifically for those that spend many hours finding creative solutions for their clients within Relativity workspaces. This certificate is also considered a great first exam to take on your way to Relativity Expert and Master

Recommended Prerequisites

At least 6 Months of experience managing projects. It's recommended to take 'Relativity 101', 'Processing', and Analytics and 'Active Learning' training

More Information

Relativity Infrastructure Specialist

The Relativity Infrastructure exam is notorious among students for being very difficult, mostly because of the high level of detail you are expected to be knowledgeable about when it comes to infrastructure. Be prepared!

Recommended Prerequisites

At least one year of experience as an infrastructure specialist/manager. It's recommended to take 'Relativity Infrastructure' training

More Information

Relativity Processing Specialist

If you ingest data into your Relativity workspaces on a daily basis then this certification is for you. Expect detailed questions about workflow and best practices.

Recommended Prerequisites

At least 6 Months of experience hands-on experience processing data. It's recommended to take ''Processing' training

More Information

Relativity Review Management Specialist

Relativity Review Management Specialist (formerly Relativity Certified User) exam is the go-to certification for review team leads or project managers to test proficiency in using the platform to ensure efficient and smooth reviews.

Recommended Prerequisites

It's recommended to take the RelativityOne Overview online class.

Relativity Certified Training Pro

Relativity Certified Training Pro (RCTP) is a new and very rigorous certification project to become a Relativity trainer.
It's the only certificate from Relativity which required mandatory prerequisites:

  • One year of training/facilitation experience
  • A valid Relativity Certified Administrator certification
  • A sponsoring company

Once accepted you will have to attend a virtual meet-and-greet session and complete six hours of pre-work for a weeklong bootcamp.

During the bootcamp you will learn the following:

  • The fundamentals of adult education
  • Best practice facilitation methods for classroom training
  • Techniques required for clear technical instruction
  • How to apply evaluation methods and give constructive feedback in a supportive environment

Having completed the program you are allowed to host Relativity 101 training yourself, for half the cost of a standard Relativity training.
