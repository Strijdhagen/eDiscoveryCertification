Veritas Certification

Veritas is a leader in data protection, backup, and recovery services for many years and includes eDiscovery in their portfolio as well. The company currently has one certificate for you to obtain

Veritas VCS-413, Veritas Certified Specialist

To obtain the "VCS-413: Administration of Veritas eDiscovery Platform 8.2 for Administrators" exam, you will need to demonstrate an understanding of installation, configuration, deployment, administration, management, maintenance, and troubleshooting of Veritas eDiscovery 8.2 Platform.
