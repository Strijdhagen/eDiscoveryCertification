Nuix Certification

Nuix has been a leader in eDiscovery for some time now, especially with their processing platform Nuix Workstation. Since acquiring Review platform Ringtail in 2018, Nuix has been one of the leading End-to-End eDiscovery providers with Nuix Discover. Nuix currently offers seven courses, three for Nuix Workstation and four for Nuix Discover. Acquire all exams of either track to become a Nuix Certified Master!

Nuix Workstation Data Discovery Core & Specialist

Nuix Workstation Data Discovery Core is a one-day introductory hands-on certification training course that teaches students new to Nuix Workstation the foundational knowledge including creating cases, data processing, searching, and reviewing. This class is ideal for users new to Nuix including litigation support staff, vendor data analysts, project managers, and litigation analysts.

The Nuix Workstation Data Discovery Specialist is a two-day hands-on certification course focusing on case data processing, early case assessment, deduplication, searching and analysis, email threading, production sets, and exports which will show you the practical steps of determining the relevancy of collected information. This course is intended for advanced Nuix users who would like to become more efficient with Nuix Workstation

More Information

Certificate Overview

  • Pricing

    $2,495 for both courses. An exam without a course is $300. It's also possible to include the 'Administrator' exam for $3,295 total.

  • Course Setup

    A 1 day-course for "Core" and a 2-day course for the "Specialist" certificate

  • Exam Duration

    The Core exam is 3 hours with 80 questions. The Nuix Specialist exam is 3 hours with 90 questions

  • Online or Testing Center?

    Online

  • Maintaining the certificate

    The certifications are valid for two years

  • Retake Policy

    After registration, you will have 90 days and two opportunities to pass the exam at 80% to receive your certification.
