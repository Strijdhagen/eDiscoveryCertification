Zylab Certification

Zylab has been in the business of eDiscovery since 1983! The Dutch company have been the pioneers of the industry ever since, and have recently released their cloud product Zylab ONE. Zylab currently has two certificates for you to obtain!

Zylab Certified Review Specialist

The ZyLAB Certified Legal Review Training program provides experienced users of the ZyLAB Legal Review System with the necessary skills to become a Certified ZyLAB Legal Review Specialist.
